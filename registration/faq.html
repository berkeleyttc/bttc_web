Registration FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Round Robin online registration at Berkeley Table Tennis Club.

When is registration open?

Registration typically opens on Wednesday at midnight (12:00 AM PST) and closes on Friday at 6:45 PM PST. The exact times are displayed on the registration page status banner.

You can only register during this window. If registration is closed, the status banner will show when it will next open.

How do I register for Round Robin?

If you're a returning player with an online account:

  1. Go to the Registration page
  2. Enter your phone number (the one associated with your account)
  3. Select yourself from the list of players found
  4. Click "Register" and complete the registration form
  5. Select your payment method and accept the liability waiver

If you're a new member or don't have an online account yet, see the question below about signing up.

I can't find my phone number / "No player found" error

If you see a "No player found" message, it means your phone number isn't in our system yet. This usually happens if:

  • You're a new member who hasn't signed up online
  • Your phone number isn't associated with your player account
  • You're using a different phone number than the one on file

Solution: You need to sign up for an online account first. This is a one-time process that activates your online player account using your phone number.

If you believe your phone number should be in the system, contact BTTC support at 510-926-6913 (text only).

How do I sign up for the first time?

If you're a returning player who hasn't activated your online account yet:

  1. Go to the Sign Up page
  2. Enter your phone number and personal information
  3. Complete the signup process
  4. Once activated, you can use the Registration page to register for events

If you're a completely new member to BTTC, you'll need to attend in person first. See the main homepage for information about new member registration.

What payment methods are accepted?

You can choose from two payment methods when registering:

  • Pay by Cash: Pay in person when you arrive at the event
  • Pay by Zelle/Venmo (recommended): Pay digitally before the event

Fees: $8.00 for adults (17+), $5.00 for juniors (16 and under) and seniors (60+). Free for players with BTTC or USATT rating over 2150.

What is the liability waiver?

All players must accept the liability waiver to register. The waiver is a standard legal document that protects both you and the club.

Note: If you are a minor, please have your parent or guardian review the waiver before you accept it.

You can read the full waiver by clicking the link in the registration form. You must check the acceptance box to complete registration.

How do I unregister?

If you need to cancel your registration:

  1. Go to the Registration page
  2. Enter your phone number and find your player account
  3. If you're already registered, you'll see an "Unregister" button
  4. Click "Unregister" and optionally provide a reason

Important: You can only unregister while event registrations are open. If registrations are closed, you'll need to contact BTTC support.

What if registration is full?

If you see a message that registration is full, it means all available spots have been taken. The capacity banner on the registration page shows how many spots are available.

Priority order: Online registrations are processed first, followed by text registrations, then in-person sign-ups.

If you believe there's an error with the capacity count, contact BTTC support at 510-926-6913 (text only).

How do I view who's registered?

You can view the current roster of registered players by clicking the "View Players Registered for Round Robin" button on the registration page, or by visiting the Roster page directly.

The roster shows all confirmed registrations and is updated in real-time as players register or unregister.

I registered but don't see my name on the roster

If you successfully completed registration but don't see your name:

  • Wait a moment: The roster may take a few seconds to update
  • Refresh the roster page: Try refreshing the roster page to see the latest updates
  • Check your registration status: Look up your phone number again on the registration page to confirm your status

If you still don't see your name after a few minutes, contact BTTC support at 510-926-6913 (text only).

Can I register multiple players?

Yes! If multiple players are associated with the same phone number, you'll see all of them when you search. You can register or unregister each player individually.

To register another player with a different phone number, you'll need to search using their phone number, or they can register themselves.

I'm getting an error message

Common errors and solutions:

  • "Registration is currently closed": Check the status banner for when registration opens next
  • "No player found": You need to sign up for an online account first
  • "Registration is full": All spots have been taken. Check back later or contact support
  • "Event registrations are currently closed": The event is not accepting registrations at this time
  • Network/connection errors: Check your internet connection and try again

If you continue to experience issues, contact BTTC support at 510-926-6913 (text only).

I need help - who do I contact?

For registration issues or questions:

BTTC Support: 510-926-6913 (text only, no voicemail)

Please include your name and a brief description of your issue when contacting support.

For general club information, visit the About page or check the main homepage.

What if I'm a new member without a rating?

If you're a completely new member to BTTC without a rating, you'll need to register in person first. See the main homepage for details about new member registration.

After your first event, you'll be able to sign up for an online account and use the online registration system for future events.
