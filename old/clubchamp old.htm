ANNOUNCING THE  BTTC CLUB CHAMPIONSHIP ON FRIDAY OCTOBER 19

On this special round robin event there will be 10 seven-player divisions.

All players MUST pre-register at least one full week before the tourney.. Oct 12 is the final sign-up deadline!!

Only the first seventy players who register will be allowed to play.. first come.. first play!!

Trophies will be awarded to the first and second place in each division (tie breaks may determine winner in some cases, Games won may be crucial in

determining  if you win a trophy, so all games are important. If games are equal then head to head result is second tie break)

We will have special starting times , pay attention to these details…the club will open at 5:45 for set-up  and warm-up and close at 10 :15 (last game at 9:55)so we will have a little extra time .

The first Matches will begin at  6 :30 but I will allow a 6 :45  match starting time for three people in each division …(again first come first serve on a special late reservation) please I cannot have  too many late starters as we must finish in one night, if you cannot make arrangements to start early on one special night please skip this event! ENTRANTS MUST BE READY TO START ON TIME OR RISK DEFAULT!

Entry Fee $6 for adults $4 /16 and under

Sign –up early, I expect this event to fill up quickly, ratings from October 12 results will be used. Sandbagging will be duly noted.

Entries are non-refundable. After the event is fully subscribed I will allow a short waiting list for last minute drop-outs although I hope we don’t have any!

Regular round robin rules apply, best out of five games, 11 point games. Maximum 90 second warm-up before each match.

 

Players must have a BTTC rating to play in this event. That is easy to get, just come and play once before the main event.
