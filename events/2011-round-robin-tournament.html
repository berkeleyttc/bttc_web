BTTC 2011 Round-Robin Tournament

The 2011 BTTC Championship Round-Robin Tournament will be held on November 18, 2011. We have changed the reward for the 1st place in each group. Please read the details below. Entries are limited. Please register and reserve your spot as early as possible.

Date and Time November 18, 2011 from 6:30 PM to 10:00 PM
Venue James Kenney Recreation Center
Entry Limit 54 players (first-come, first-served)
Entry Deadline November 11, 2011
Format Round-robin with 9 divisions of different levels (at least 5 players in each division)
Registration Fee $8.00 for juniors (17 and younger) and $10.00 for adults. The registration fee is due by the entry deadline (November 11, 2011)
Withdrawal Any player who wishes to withdraw from the event must do so prior to October 31, 2011. There will be no refunds after this date
Ratings Division assignment will be based on BTTC rating as of November 11, 2011

How to register

Players must register with David Bernstein before the entry deadline. Three methods of registration are available:

  • Onsite during October's Friday round-robins;
  • Email Dave Bernstein
  • Text (510) 316-5108

Rewards for each division

1st place Trophy plus 5 free nights of Friday round-robin
2nd place Trophy

Tournament rules

Above the standard USATT rules, the following rules apply to the tournament:

  • Players must be members of the BTTC club (with an existing rating)
  • Coaching is not allowed during matches
  • Additional rules to be applied will be announced prior to the tournament

For more information, contact Dave Bernstein
